Welcome !

Hello there !

 

I am an accomplished Modeling Research Fellow and Scientific Dissemination Technical Leader at Novadiscovery with a strong background in biomathematics and mathematical modeling. With extensive experience as a Post-Doctoral Researcher and Research Engineer, I have contributed significantly to the field through my work on analyzing data, developing mathematical models, and effectively communicating research findings through publications and presentations. My expertise lies in utilizing mathematical models, specifically Ordinary Differential Equations (ODEs), to understand complex biological systems, particularly in the context of cancer dynamics.

 

Throughout my career, I have demonstrated a consistent track record of planning and executing projects aligned with organizational goals and driving positive business impact. I know how to navigate uncertain and challenging situations while ensuring accountability and delivering exceptional results. Moreover, my leadership skills extend to building diverse and high-performing teams, facilitating discussions on sensitive topics, and optimizing resource allocation to achieve common objectives. With a passion for scientific dissemination, I actively contribute to conferences and publications, fostering collaboration and knowledge exchange within the scientific community.

 

If you are looking for a dedicated professional who combines strong technical expertise in biomathematics with effective leadership and communication skills... Let's explore how we can collaborate to advance scientific research and make a meaningful impact in our respective fields !
